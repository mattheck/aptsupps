BCAAs - 5,000 mg

BCAAs consist of three amino acids: Leucine, Isoleucine and Valine. These are all Essential Amino Acids (EAA) meaning your body cannot make them and thus required from your diet. BCAAs are especially important in your diet due to their roles in muscle mass regulation.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="3681"]

Black Pepper Fruit Extract (BioPerine®) - 5 mg

Black pepper Fruit Extract is made from the Piper nigrum plant. SUPERSET™ uses a full 5 mg of the exact dose validated through numerous clinical studies to help support increased absorption of other ingredients by as much as 2,000%.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="3676"]

L-Citrulline - 3,000 mg

When orally supplemented, L-Citrulline converts to L-Arginine in the kidneys, which leads to increased L-Arginine and L-Ornithine plasma content. L-Citrulline has become a popular alternative to supplementing with Arginine because in the liver and intestines Arginine experiences excessive breakdown while L-Citrulline does not.

Beta-Alanine - 2,000 mg

Beta-Alanine is a key ingredient for supporting muscular endurance. Elevated intracellular L-Carnosine levels can serve as a “buffer” to the acidic environment created by exercise. Beta-Alanine has become a pre-workout “must-have”, and is found in SUPERSET™ a full dose of 2,000 mg.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="3679"]

Caffeine Anhydrous - 300 mg

Caffeine is an adenosine receptor antagonist. When a receptor is acted upon by adenosine, a sense of relaxation and sedation occurs. By inhibiting the receptor, caffeine promotes acute wakefulness and alertness.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="4010"]

L-Tyrosine - 1,500 mg

L-Tyrosine is an amino acid that is said to have anti-stress and cognition supporting properties, as it plays a role in noradrenaline and dopamine production. Often included in pre-workout formulas because of its ability to help support improved mood, focus, alertness and energy during training. It has been reported to work well in combination with caffeine.

N-Acetyl L-Cysteine - 750 mg

N-Acetyl L-Cysteine is metabolized into L-Cysteine when consumed, which may support Glutathione production. Through “Glutathione Buffering,” NAC may have antioxidative properties. Research has shown that regular use of N-Acetyl L-Cysteine prior to training has resulted in delayed muscle fatigue and helped support muscle endurance and strength.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="3680"]

Creatine Monohydrate - 3,000 mg

Creatine can support performance and muscle building goals like strength, size and endurance. SUPERSET™ features concentrated anhydrous  creatine, which is 6% purer than monohydrate with improved solubility and absorption.

[/text-with-icon][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="20"][text-with-icon icon_type="image_icon" icon_image="3966"]

Betaine Anhydrous - 1,500 mg

Betaine, also known as Trimethylglycine, may support cardiovascular health. When taken orally, Betaine serves as a methyl donor in the body, whether to convert (re-methylate) Homocysteine into Methionine, or indirectly support S-Adenosyl Methionine (SAMe) and Folate methylation.
